Nintendo Switch Outsells NES

Tyson Rodriguez

The Nintendo switch has officially passed the NES in sales. Which means that it is now Nintendo's third most-popular console, right behind the SNES and the Original Wii. The switch has definetly been one of my favorite consoles in the past decade. This news is exciting news for Nintendo and im excited on more they have to offer.

Outer Worlds for the Switch will not have physical copies

Tyson Rodriguez

Outer Worlds, by Obsidian games, is a wonderful game. It is no secret as to why the company will port it over to the Nintendo Switch. However, Outer Worlds for the switch will not have a physical cartridge and will instead have only download codes. I think this is a good idea, however, many consumers wonder why. I think the move to digital games, is a good one. It not only helps the environment, but also shifts to a more digital age.

Undertale REVIEW

Tyson Rodriguez

After my first run through of Undertale, I found myself realizing that I am a horrible person. That was when I found out that I can play the game without huting anyone or killing everyone.
